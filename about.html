Our Journey

From a small startup to a global technology brand

2015

The Beginning

FutureShop was founded in a small garage by two tech enthusiasts with a vision to revolutionize home technology.

2017

Expansion

After rapid growth, we moved to our first office and expanded our team to 15 dedicated professionals.

2019

International Launch

FutureShop went global, expanding to 10 countries and launching our first proprietary smart home product line.

2022

Innovation Hub

We opened our state-of-the-art Innovation Center, dedicated to researching and developing the next generation of smart home technology.

Today

Leading the Future

Today, FutureShop is a leading global brand with over 50,000 customers in 25 countries, continuing our mission to bring the future home.
